Our Story

Little Italy in Marrakech

Founded in 2010, Little Italy brings the authentic taste of Italian cuisine to the heart of Marrakech. Our restaurant combines traditional Italian recipes with local Moroccan ingredients to create a unique dining experience.

Our head chef has trained in Naples and Rome, bringing decades of experience in authentic Italian cooking to every dish we serve.
